About Us - Green Edge Mowing

The story behind Green Edge Mowing and the local teenagers building it.

Green Edge Mowing was founded by two local teenagers, Jake and Thomas, who wanted to make it easier — and cheaper — for people in our community to keep their yards looking great. What started as a simple idea between two mates has grown into a reliable, youth-powered mowing service built on hard work, honesty, and genuine community spirit.

As teenagers, we’re keen to prove that young people can deliver real value. We show up on time, put in the effort, and take pride in every lawn we mow and every edge we sharpen. Our goal is to offer a service that’s affordable for families, neighbours, and anyone who needs a hand, without cutting corners on quality.

Green Edge Mowing isn’t just a business — it’s a way for us to learn, contribute, and give back to the community that raised us. When you support us, you’re supporting young locals who are eager to help, eager to work, and eager to make a difference.

Your lawn gets the edge. Your community gets stronger.
