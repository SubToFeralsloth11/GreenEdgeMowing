About Jake

The original mind behind Green Edge Mowing and a driving force behind the business.

Jake is the original mind behind Green Edge Mowing — the one who came up with the idea, shaped the structure of the business, and turned a simple thought into something real. Without his drive and vision, Green Edge Mowing wouldn’t exist in the first place.

From day one, Jake has been focused on creating a service that’s affordable, reliable, and genuinely helpful for the community. He planned out how the business would run, organised the workflow, and set the standard for the quality and effort that Green Edge Mowing is known for. His leadership keeps everything running smoothly, whether it’s scheduling jobs, managing equipment, or making sure every lawn gets the attention it deserves.

Jake brings energy, initiative, and a strong work ethic to the team. He’s the kind of person who sees a need and steps up to fill it — and that’s exactly how Green Edge Mowing was born. His goal is simple: provide great service, help the community, and build something meaningful alongside his best mate.
