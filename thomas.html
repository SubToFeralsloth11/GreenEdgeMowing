About Thomas

A co-founder of Green Edge Mowing and the builder behind the website.

Thomas is one of the co-founders of Green Edge Mowing and the creative mind behind our website. When he’s not out mowing lawns, he’s busy designing and developing software, building games, and exploring new ideas in the world of technology. His passion for coding started early, and he’s been creating small games, tools, and digital projects ever since.

Bringing those skills into the business, Thomas designed and built the Green Edge Mowing website from the ground up — making it simple, clean, and easy for customers to get in touch. He loves combining creativity with problem-solving, whether that’s writing code or making sure a lawn looks sharp.

Thomas is proof that young people can do a bit of everything: run a business, support the community, and build real software at the same time. His goal is to keep improving both the tech side of Green Edge Mowing and the quality of service we deliver.
